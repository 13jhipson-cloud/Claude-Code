# Key Career Advice

CIMA is a good yardstick for when to stick/shift. its a race to get through CIMA then think about the best options

1. **Lean into AI:** Learn about AI, Keep up to date with the latest tools and models, Use AI to automate workflows and free up time for myself, BE AI NATIVE!
2. **Use freed up time to:** Make things happen in the business, Partner more closely with sales and operations teams, Get more C-suite exposure, Talk to human beings, not screens.
3. **Get on cross-functional projects:** Work across different departments, Learn to speak the language of the business (not just the numbers)
4. **Get experience in financial controlling (accounting & reporting)**: Make sure I properly understand the accounting and month-end process (hands on finance and accounting experience)
5. **Learn storytelling:** Focus on developing my soft skills such as storytelling & communicating
